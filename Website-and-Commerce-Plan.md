# Website And Commerce Plan

## Website Goal

Create a simple place to show CalCo Prints products, explain the story, and eventually sell or take orders.

## Early Website Sections

- Home.
- Products.
- About CalCo Prints.
- How products are made.
- Custom or limited drops, only if offered.
- Contact or order interest form.

## Product Listing Needs

Each listing should include:

- Product name.
- Product photos.
- Short character description.
- What is included.
- Size.
- Material.
- Color options.
- Price.
- Safety/age note if needed.
- Shipping or pickup details.

## Ecommerce Options To Consider Later

- Simple static website with contact form.
- Shopify.
- Etsy.
- Square.
- Local/in-person sales first, website as catalog.

## Website Risks

- Selling online may require parent/guardian account setup.
- Shipping adds cost, packaging requirements, and customer service.
- Sales tax rules may apply.
- Custom orders can become hard to manage.

## Best Early Move

Build the product and packaging system first, then create a clean catalog page once the first few products are real.
