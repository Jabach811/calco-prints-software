Every print starts as an idea.

A sketch, a character, a thing that doesn't exist yet. Then the printer gets to work.

Built layer by layer.

Hundreds of layers of melted plastic, stacked with robot precision. It's basically a hot glue gun with a brain.

Checked. Packaged. Ready.

Every piece gets inspected before it goes out. If it's not cool enough to gift, it doesn't ship.
