# Automation Ideas

Do not automate too early. First, learn the real workflow. Then automate repeated steps.

## Useful Future Automations

### Product Spec To Listing

Turn a product spec into:

- Website listing copy.
- Packaging copy.
- Social post copy.
- Price checklist.

### Prototype Log To QA Checklist

Turn prototype notes into:

- Known print issues.
- Final slicer notes.
- Ready-to-sell checklist.

### Sales Tracker Summary

Summarize:

- Products sold.
- Revenue.
- Cost.
- Estimated profit.
- Money split by bucket.

### Inventory Tracker

Track:

- Finished products.
- Packaged products.
- Filament stock.
- Packaging stock.
- Products needing reprint.

### Photo Workflow

Rename and organize product photos by:

- Product.
- Version.
- Color.
- Date.
- Use case.

## Automation Rule

If a task happens once, do it manually. If it happens three times and the steps are stable, consider making a template or automation.
